# 102 - Logging Into OnDemand and SSH
#### [Made by CS 8803 MGT & Leadership GT with Scribe](https://scribehow.com/o/RwVvEgxYTayBbEjBpzDTfg/viewer/102_-_Logging_Into_OnDemand_and_SSH__K99yzAkMQlG3ONpwbcedXw)
Learn how to connect to the PACE ICE high-performance computing environment through GlobalProtect and Open OnDemand. This guide walks you through authenticating your connection and accessing the web-based portal to manage your computational jobs.

#### VPN Connection


1\. Open GlobalProtect from the menu bar.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-18/d5a95b79-74d6-487d-953a-7ea14c13cc05/sps-redacted-ed20a4ecb8544a06b15c78e0327bc875_12699268d0354391b11ef171554579ba_text_export.jpeg)


2\. Click "Connect".

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-18/89188a50-2dd0-4d66-abd1-9c15387d1ed9/sps-redacted-72d2ced5684444b39c320b7636f90783_71b10a3e965745baa37bdfe1b0f67fdd_text_export.jpeg)


3\. Enter your password in GlobalProtect.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-18/80fe1071-64ab-44ac-81c4-f2d8a388220c/sps-redacted-ee4729cb4f9e492fb21bdb2acc53fe03_630b05072ea44cafa81ad8df6c466f0b_text_export.jpeg)

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-18/7762dfa7-16af-4da4-b4ab-32beafa4d489/sps-redacted-e351f2f1efb44686b1cd33e667957d49_71489f37137e4ddf9f80665ea153956b_text_export.jpeg)


4\. Click "Connect" again to establish the VPN connection.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-18/3a512870-3ae2-4e7d-96c1-dd88ca8935a5/sps-redacted-31ef7133ffcd4475893c975c95abe7f3_44785e26b5144057a48338b910100071_text_export.jpeg)


#### Server Access via Terminal


5\. Search for "Terminal" and open the application.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-18/a28894de-585a-4c5a-946d-107643499537/sps-redacted-26a2e0c449364b53b585b3361850a955_112933e25a6c45f6a51f64af62d052b4_text_export.jpeg)

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-18/e80358e7-7415-4118-a5a5-37e2cea5a93b/sps-redacted-421d865018024017afdf0e1053828740_f35ffc812eae472ba5b07f4d0b2f7f53_text_export.jpeg)


6\. Type the SSH command: `ssh username@login-ice.pace.gatech.edu` and press Enter.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-18/2eedc4d2-32a5-4783-9da3-8a91d2c1bf64/sps-redacted-39f16bc59756486e873afbf440bc5f40_85fff87f417645a3ab403b2556fcb4c6_text_export.jpeg)


7\. Type your password in the terminal window when prompted. Note: The password should be identical to your Buzzport password.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-18/aa6511ee-51b8-4179-95c4-0ff20ecc4917/sps-redacted-097e5e5311ce42589f770b8ef4986994_9bd3dff86c9842848af3b9d39e3ea4e9_text_export.jpeg)


8\. If you are successful, it should populate like so!

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-18/4e0b505f-4573-429a-9523-d5342bc5e49c/sps-redacted-310820f576934394bbd1d744db1d9b00_8071d626fda54290b6fd420ecb242882_text_export.jpeg)


#### OnDemand Portal Navigation


9\. To create Jobs and use GUI services, connect via [https://ondemand-\
ice.pace.gatech.edu/](https://ondemand-ice.pace.gatech.edu/) in your browser.
